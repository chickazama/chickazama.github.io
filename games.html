Games

A collection of games I made using Scratch. Click the green flag to start.

Asteroid Smash

Objective: Destroy the asteroids, collect power ups. Survive for as long as you can.

Controls: Move your cursor to move the spaceship. Left-click to fire bullets.

Cube Field

Objective: Dodge the oncoming cubes. Survive as long as you can.

Controls: Left/Right arrow keys to dodge oncoming cubes.

Curve Mania (Achtung Die Kurve/Curve Fever re-make) - Local Multiplayer

Objective: Draw a snake to destroy your enemies. Avoid crashing into other snakes/the wall.

Controls: See the start menu screen for keyboard controls for each player.
